Bonjour
Meaning: Hello / Good morning
Word Breakdown: Bon (good) + jour (day) ‚Üí "Good day"
Grammar Tip: "Bonjour" is used with both men and women, and works in formal and informal settings.
Culture Tip: In French culture, always greet with "Bonjour" when entering shops, restaurants, or meeting someone for the first time.
Example: Bonjour, comment allez-vous ? English: Hello, how are you?
